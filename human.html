Human Resources Policy

Human Resources at Healthy Food is responsible for employee recruitment, career development, training, payroll and other administrative operations.

At Healthy Food, employee experience is at the heart of Human Resources strategy. Tukas Human Resources' innovative, solution and high quality focused career planning system aims to develop employees' knowledge, capability and competency and prepare them for the next level in their careers. In accordance with Healthy Food employee promotion policy, employees with high potential are determined and prepared for more roles with greater responsibilities.

Human Resources carries out administrative personnel operations on time and to a high quality. The employees are encouraged to submit their worries about non-ethical and illegal issues to the management.

Healthy Food, being an active company in the food sector, focuses on maintaining a healthy and safe workplace for its employees and keeps improving its standards.
