Ethic Rules and Social Responsibility

The Company’s ethic rules are being declared to the public through its web site. At the use of the missions, authority and responsibilities that are defined at the Company; it has been accepted as a main principle to act according to the Articles of Association of the Company with Turkish Food Codex, Turkish Trade Code, Debits Law, Import Regime and Regulations, Customs Law, Foreign Exchange Regime, Tax Procedure Law and concerning regulations, Capital Market Law and the Labor Law that is organizing the business life and the law order that is valid and effective at our country.

As it has been mentioned at our “Mission and Vision”; as being adopted to the understand of total quality, it is our main principle not to waive from mutual confidence, prevailing business ethics and honest behaviors principle at our commercial and social relationships when realizing our aims and keeping up to the social responsibility and environment conscious.
