Quality and Food Safety

Our aim is to become a global brand by manufacturing products that fully meet consumer needs and demands.

We believe that we will achive our aim by working and growing together with our farmers and retailers, increasing our efficiency and profitability and applying a total quality management approach.

We want to be an innovative company that respects law and order, environmentally friendly in production, supports consumer rights and awareness, adds value to our shareholders and the community and continuously applies social responsibility knowledge.

We commit ourselves working towards ‘consumer satisfaction’ and pledge to work and constantly improve our working system in this direction.
