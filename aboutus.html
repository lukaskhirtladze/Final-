About Us

Healthy Food produces a wide spectrum of products manufactured with modern systems, such as tomato paste, canned products (vegetables, delicatessen, pulses, ready meals), pickled vegetables, jams, sauces (ketchup, mayonnaise, hot pepper sauce, breakfeast souce) and frozen products

OUR VISION

As a leading company in the food industry, our vision is to: adopt a customer focused management system, accept the total quality production concept, constantly improve technology, become a ‘major food brand’ and ‘world-wide known brand’ by having a wide spectrum of products and extensive exporting

OUR MISSION

We want to be known as a company that

  • Constantly creates value to our customers, our workers, our partners and to the community
  • Respects law and order and business ethics
  • Uses resources efficiently
  • Produces quality products, is trustworthy and constantly improves upon itself
  • Values its employees, encourages teamwork and participation and provides a good working environment where talents can thrive
  • Is conscious of the environment and responsive to the public
  • Grows and aims to grow with the input of the suppliers
  • Adopts modern management techniques
  • Providing sustainable and profitable growth, competitive.

OUR VALUES

Our common values in line with our mission and values which shape our behaviour, decisions and aims in work are:

  • Customer satisfaction
  • Continuation of product quality
  • Cost awareness, work efficiency
  • Staff health and health and safety at work
  • People first
  • Effective and widespread communication
  • Personal achievement
  • Business ethics
  • Creativeness, entrepreneurship
  • Continous development innovation
  • Sharing
  • Environmental health
